### 13. Consistency check (`consistency_check.md`)

**Three structural issues found in original — all addressed:**

**Issue 1 — Major duplication (Chapters 6 and 8):**
Chapter 8 contains a near-verbatim copy of the Chapter 6 Thorne debate, including the same flashback to graduate school, the same dialogue exchanges, and the same private message ("I was wrong. You were right. I'm sorry."). This appears to be an accidental duplication from drafting rather than intentional repetition.

*Resolution:* Chapter 8 now replaces the duplicate with new material — Anya reflecting on *what she learned* from the debate (the difference between winning publicly and winning actually), followed by her receiving the Lin raid alert. The Thorne storyline is referenced but not re-narrated. The chapter now carries its own weight and functions as a bridge to the action climax.

**Issue 2 — Tao's pronoun (Chapter 9):**
Tao is established as female in Chapters 3 ("her wrinkled face," "her eyes") and introduced via her grandson calling her "Grandmother." Chapter 9 switched to "His eyes, closed in meditation."

*Resolution:* Corrected to "Her eyes."

**Issue 3 — Dropped cliffhanger (Chapters 10–11):**
Chapter 10 ends with Vance's urgent warning: "Anya, wait! Don't do it! It's a trap!" Chapter 11 opens with the neural link proceeding as if this warning never happened, with no acknowledgment, decision, or consequence.

*Resolution:* Chapter 11 now opens with Anya consciously holding and then dismissing the warning, converting a plot hole into a character choice: "If it was a trap, the trap had already closed. And if it wasn't — she had to find out. She let it go."
